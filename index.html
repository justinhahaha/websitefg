About Us

FG Forwarding & Transport Services Sdn Bhd, established on December 19, 1994, embodies the vision and expertise of its founder, Mr. Felix Francis Goonting. With a profound understanding of the road transport and forwarding industry, Mr. Goonting has crafted a company philosophy centered on excellence, reliability, and innovation.

Our commitment to these core values drives us to continuously deliver superior logistics solutions, fostering trust and satisfaction among our clients. We strive to set new benchmarks in the logistics sector by upholding the principles of efficiency, customer-centric service, and sustainable practices.
