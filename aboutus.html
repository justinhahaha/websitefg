Philosophy

FG Forwarding & Transport Services Sdn Bhd, established on December 19, 1994, embodies the vision and expertise of its founder, Mr. Felix Francis Goonting. With a profound understanding of the road transport and forwarding industry, Mr. Goonting has crafted a company philosophy centered on excellence, reliability, and innovation. Our commitment to these core values drives us to continuously deliver superior logistics solutions, fostering trust and satisfaction among our clients. We strive to set new benchmarks in the logistics sector by upholding the principles of efficiency, customer-centric service, and sustainable practices.

Vision

To revolutionize the logistics industry by setting the highest standards for reliability, innovation, and customer satisfaction, becoming the go-to logistics partner nationwide.

Mission

  • Innovative Solutions: Continuously develop and implement cutting-edge logistics solutions to enhance efficiency and effectiveness in supply chain management.
  • Customer Satisfaction: Prioritize customer satisfaction by delivering reliable, timely, and cost-effective services.

All trading will be subjected to the Federation of Malaysian Freight Forwarders Standard Trading Conditions (STC).
